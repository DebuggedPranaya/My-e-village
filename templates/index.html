Solar energy

Village-wide solar panel installations across public buildings, streetlights, and a community solar farm powering over 300 households with clean renewable energy.

300+ households Solar streetlights Community farm
Active Since 2021

Smart roads

Upgraded internal road network with reflective lane markings, solar-powered road studs, and traffic sensors at key junctions to improve safety and flow for all commuters.

14 km upgraded Road studs Traffic sensors
Ongoing Since 2022

Sanitation & drainage

End-to-end underground drainage network across all wards, with household toilet coverage reaching 98% and a centralised waste water treatment plant serving the entire village.

98% coverage Treatment plant All 9 wards

"Our village has stepped into the digital age. Through e-Village, government services can now reach every household."
