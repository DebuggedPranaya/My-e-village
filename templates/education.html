Skill development

Vocational training in Wai

Practical skills training programmes available at the Gram Panchayat centre and nearby institutes for youth, women, and farmers.

Tailoring & stitching

Mahila Gat, Wai

3-month certificate course for women. Covers basic stitching, blouse making, and salwar suit. Batches run twice a year at the GP centre.

Agri-business management

Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Pune

6-day residential workshop on crop planning, market linkage, and post-harvest handling. Free for registered farmers of Mahu.

Electrical & solar repair

ITI Haveli, Pune

Short-term trade course on basic wiring, solar panel installation, and maintenance. Govt-certified. Transport support from Mahu available.

Basic computer operations

GP Digital Centre, Wai

30-hour beginner course covering MS Office, internet, email, and govt portal usage. Available in Marathi. Batches every month.
