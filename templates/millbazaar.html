Wai's farmer-owned cooperative sugar mill — supplying cane, earning fairly. Crushing season runs October through March every year.

How to register your sugarcane

1 Register on the Maha Us Nondani portal run by the Sugar Commissionerate of Maharashtra.
2 Keep ready: 7/12 extract (land ownership proof), Aadhaar card, and bank passbook.
3 Submit your survey number, sugarcane variety, estimated yield, and expected harvesting time.
4 Visit the mill's agriculture department or use their mobile app / web portal to complete factory-level registration.
