🐄

Milk & Dairy

The dominant economic sector in Wai. Organised cattle farming has transformed milk production from a subsistence activity into a major commercial enterprise. The village cooperative collects and supplies milk to Pune dairies daily.

⚙️

Agro-Processing

Small-scale cooperative-run processing units handle vermicomposting, biogas generation, and grain storage. These add value to farm output and reduce post-harvest losses — keeping more income within the village.

☀️

Renewable Energy

Wai's 100% solar status has created a new economic layer — zero electricity costs for homes and businesses, surplus energy fed back to the grid, and reduced dependency on costly diesel for irrigation and water pumping.

🎋

Sugarcane Farming

A cornerstone crop supplied to the Jawahar Shetkari Sahakari Sakhar Karkhana. The FRP rate of ₹340/quintal ensures fair compensation for farmers. Drip irrigation has improved yield per acre significantly.

🌼

Floriculture & Fruits

Marigold, jasmine, and grape cultivation provide high-value income for smallholders. Flowers are sold at Pune APMC and local weekly markets. Grapes from Wai have growing export potential through Nashik channels.

🧺

Cottage & Trade

Small home-based enterprises including tailoring, food processing (pickles, papad), and local trade. Supported by Mahila Gat and the GP's self-employment schemes.
